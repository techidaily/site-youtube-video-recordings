## How to Download Facebook Videos without Software

**Step 1.** Replace "www" with the letter "m" in the URL of the video on Facebook you want to download.  
 Example: http://www.facebook.com/...htm - http://m.facebook.com/...htm 

**Step 2.**This will bring you to the Facebook mobile site, once going there then click the Facebook video to set it play. 

**Step 3.** While the video is playing you just need to right click on the video screen and select "save video as" and rename the file also pick a location folder on your pc to save the video.
