###  Tips to Pick up Music for The Wedding Ceremony 2016 2018

Ideally, you only have one wedding in your whole life, besides choosing the right wedding dress, wedding songs come to the top that matters to the whole wedding ceremony. If you want to have a perfect, unforgettable and memorable wedding ceremony, you have to pay close attention to the best wedding songs selection. Here gives you a few tips that help you to choose the best wedding songs. 

**1\. Seek the song that only belongs to you and your partner.** The song that tied you together, a couple song that every time you listen it, no matter you are together or apart, you will think of each other. Doesn't matter if it is a proper song for wedding or not, the wedding is all about your two. Consider you guys are the center of what the whole night is about. 

**2\. Check out the lyrics of the songs.** Most songs which made themselves the wedding songs are most because of the lyrics. Reminds your experience being together, reminds the time you first met, reminds your quarrel, and reminds your beautiful memory. 

**3\. Watch out the melody.** All songs for the wedding need to be suitable for dancing, especially for slow-motion dance. It's not a party in a night club – unless you want to make it look like a night club. Choose the melody that has the playing of easy listening instruments such as violin, piano, accordion, etc. 

**4\. Take Well-Known music for wedding.** Although the wedding is all about you and your partner, you still have to pick some wedding songs that most of the guests are familiar with if you want to make them feel involved of the wedding ceremony, it is the best way to warm up the event – music is the common language to ask them dance and laugh.
